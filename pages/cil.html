Coal India Limited

The host organization of Krishi Vigyan Kendra, Ekalavya Foundation has signed a Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) with Coal India Limited on November 12, 2020. According to MoU Coal India Ltd. has approved a grant of Rs. 1,57,29,081 (Rupees One Crore Fifty-Seven Lakh Twenty-Nine Thousand Eighty-One Only) for the construction of Resource Persons & Trainers accommodation at Ekalavya Foundation Krishi Vigyan Kendra (KVK) in Medak district of Telangana State. The Resource persons building construction was completed by March -2022 and inaugurated by Directors of Ekalavya Grameena Vikas Foundation in April 2022.
