Soil Testing Lab

The soil testing lab enables us to analyze different soil parameters which gives information about the status of the sample. It helps the farmers to plan his fertilizer schedule accordingly to meet the demand of the crops.

We analyze soil sample for 12 parameters which include

  • pH
  • E.C - Electronic Conductivity
  • Organic Matter
  • Organic Carbon
  • Nitrogen
  • Phosphorus
  • Potassium
  • Sulphur
  • Zinc
  • Copper
  • Boron
  • Iron
