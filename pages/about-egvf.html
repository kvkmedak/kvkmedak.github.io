EkalavyaGrameena Vikas Foundation(EGVF)


Vision


Building a Self sustainable, Eco friendly, Integrated Farming System that produces healthy farm produce, preserves soil fertility and improves farmers' incomes.

To reduce the time lag between generation of technology at the research institutions and its transfer to the farmer's field for increasing production, productivity and income from the agriculture and allied sectors on a sustained basis with emphasis on non-chemical methods of Soil fertility and Pest management.


Mission


Promotion of natural /organic farming in villages.

Promotion of integrated agricultural practices for doubling farmers income.

To Provide skill development for rural youth, women and underprivileged sections of society and empower them.

Educate & empower the farmers, youth to develop Natural/Organic farming by creating a level playing field to provide a platform for Natural/organic farm products, through certification, traceability, marketability & supply chain mechanism.

Encourage, facilitate and assist young & women entrepreneurs to establish MSMEs.

To increase the quality of life by dissemination of knowledge about health, hygiene and nutrition.

Promotion of ancillary occupations like diary, animal husbandry, poultry, goatery etc. to maximize farmers income generation opportunities.

Special emphasis on women, marginalized sections of the society by making them part of developmental activities.
