Permanent Pandal System



It is constructed to grow cucurbitaceous and other vine type crops. Here we are growing spine gourd as this vegetable has great demand in the marker but planting materials are not available to the farmers. Turmeric crop grows well under the shade condition and cultivating as inter crop in the inter row space for effective utilization of land and to generate the extra income.
